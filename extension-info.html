About Compliance Scanner

The Compliance Scanner browser extension helps organizations detect and document security policy discrepancies between supplier policies and contractual agreements. It supports compliance with standards like ISO 27001 and NIST by flagging policy mismatches and providing actionable recommendations.

  • Real-time contract and policy comparison
  • Rule-based mismatch detection
  • Compliance mapping to ISO 27001 and NIST
  • Downloadable reports
